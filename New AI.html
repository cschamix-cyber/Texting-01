What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines, enabling them to think, learn, and make decisions. From virtual assistants to self-driving cars, AI is transforming the way we live and work.

Applications of AI

  • Healthcare: Diagnosing diseases and recommending treatments.
  • Finance: Detecting fraud and automating trading.
  • Transportation: Powering autonomous vehicles.
  • Customer Service: Chatbots that handle complex queries.
  • Education: Personalized learning recommendations.

Benefits of AI

AI offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Increased efficiency and productivity.
  • Reduction of human error.
  • Handling of dangerous tasks without risk to humans.
  • 24/7 availability for continuous operations.
